MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

The Department of Mechanical Engineering offers B.E. (Mechanical Engineering), M.E. (Manufacturing Engineering) & Ph.D. (Mechanical Engineering). The department has a Business Incubator CIPD-BI, recognized by Ministry of MSME, Govt. of India for carrying out research and consultancy projects worth Rs. 1.02 crores which resulted in 3 start-ups and 21 patents. In addition, the department is actively involved in funded research projects worth Rs.1.52 crores, received from various agencies such as AICTE, BIRAC, DRDO and DST. More than 150 research papers of faculty members have been published in refereed Journals & Conferences and the department has produced 8 Ph.D. scholars in the past three years. MoU’s have been signed with leading industries for equipping students with quality, experiential industrial knowledge and entrepreneurial skills. The department of Mechanical Engineering has produced 55 UG and 9 PG Rank holders including one Gold medal in Anna University Examinations. Presence of our alumni is felt in prominent organizations. Our students are active participants in National level innovative product development contests and have bagged cash awards to the tune ofRs.39.85 lakhs in events like AICTE-CII-i3, NDRF, NRDC, Infymaker’16, DRDO DRUSE, BIRAC SoCH’17, AICTE-ECI-ISTE ChatraVishwakarma Award’18 during the last 3 years.

MR.MACHINIST


Unleash your machining skills, and let into the groove of screeching rhythms of turning, facing, tapering and all you can do in a lathe club.

Want to perform operations on the lathe machine? This is it, an event where your show begins how strong you are in workshop practices.
DESCRIPTION
1. Participants are required to perform a set of operations on the job using lathe machine problem statement for which will be declared on the spot.

2. The competition of producing a mechanical component given in drawing by using a lathe machine. A work evaluation is done based on surface finish, the time required, and dimensional accuracy. Several operations like facing, taper turning, parting, etc will be included in the drawing.
RULES
1. Maximum of 3-members are allowed per team.

2. If any damage to the tool, lathe m/c, surrounding, etc or any malpractices including negligence during machining will lead to disqualification.

3. Fine will be levived if any major damages to Tool, lathe machine or surroundings occur during operation.

4. After the completion of the task, all the accessories must be returned.

5. No extra workpiece will be provided and carrying of any extra material is banned.

6. Avoid loose clothing, hairstyle, etc.

7. Judges decision will be final, binding to all and irrevocable.

8. The participant is responsible for their safety.

9. The winner will be decided based on accuracy, Surface finish and time duration.
